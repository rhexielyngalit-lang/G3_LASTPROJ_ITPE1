    Shipping & Return Policy

    Effective Date: 2026

    🚚 Shipping Policy

    Processing Time

    Orders are processed within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation. Orders are not processed on weekends and holidays.

    Delivery Time

    Local shipping takes approximately 3–7 business days. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and courier delays.

    Shipping Fees

    Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight.

    Order Tracking

    Once your order is shipped, a tracking number will be provided via email or message.

    📦 Return & Exchange Policy

    Return Eligibility

    • Item is defective or damaged
    • Wrong item received
    • Item must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition with tags intact

    Return Period

    Returns must be requested within 7 days of receiving your order.

    Non-Returnable Items

    • Sale or discounted items (unless defective)
    • Items that have been used, washed, or altered

    🔄 Return Process

    1. Contact our support team
    2. Provide your order details and reason for return
    3. Wait for approval and instructions
    4. Ship the item back to the provided address

    💰 Refunds

    Refunds will be processed once the returned item is received and inspected. Approved refunds will be issued via the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

    🔁 Exchanges

    Exchanges are allowed for size or item replacements, subject to availability. Customers may be responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.

    📩 Contact Us

    If you have any questions regarding shipping or returns, please contact us through our website’s contact page.
