    FAQs

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept Cash on Delivery, GCash, PayMaya, and Bank Transfer.

    How long does shipping take?

    Orders are usually delivered within 3–7 business days.

    Can I return or exchange items?

    Yes, returns and exchanges are allowed within 7 days after receiving your order.

    How can I track my order?

    You can check your order status in the “My Orders” section.

    Do you offer discounts?

    Yes! Check our SALE section for ongoing promos and discounts.
